Welcome to aei 

Aggarwal Engineering Inc. (AEI) is a network of experienced professionals which helps manufacturers obtainaffordable, on-time product approvals from various safety and regulatory agencies. The company specializes in Alternative Energy, Electric Vehicles , Switchgear, Power and Control products but can providesassistance and support toall electrical products and device manufacturers who are looking to enter any target market worldwide. We can help determine the rules, regulations, standards and any ther mandatory requirements, their product need to comply with and how they go about getting it all done. Our specialties also include help with Quality Systems and assistance and training for CE Mark. For further details please see our complete list of services. All of our associates have more than 30 years of experience working in both international regulatory agencies and industry. We are best positioned to provide answers to the regulatory issues and help meet your business needs. 

We are headquartered in Vancouver Canada but have associates located throughout the West Coast, the United States, Canada and abroad. We will help you get into any market in the shortest possible time and in the most economical way possible.
